Our group is interested in solving problems that are related to climate change. Every Drop is a website that serves as a forum for shared ideas, tools, and resources to help water conservation. They provide tips on how people can do to save water in their daily lives. Nevertheless, because it mainly provides location-based information, it can limit their target audience. In addition, the website only focuses on water conservation, but we want to expand the dimensions of climate change to more people.
